About
Leonard Abraham is a Los Angeles-based cinematographer with a background in camera operating and first assistant camera work. His images are guided by character, atmosphere, and a search for emotional clarity. He works across narrative, commercial, and music-driven projects, bringing a tactile and story-centered approach to light, framing, and movement.
